What is CafeNeatza?

Is CafeaNeatza a website for coffee? No, CafeaNeatza is a lifestyle created to gather all coffee lovers in a united community that is respected and has the best experience!


What does CafeNeatza do?

CafeNeatza deals with the creation of a united and happy community, but also with the manufacture and distribution of coffee.


Who founded CafeNeatza? and why?

The community was created by the CEO of the company Matei Alexandru in 2022 to be able to bring the best coffee to the whole world and to make people happy.
